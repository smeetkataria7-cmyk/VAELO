# DVOC Institute — Lead Generation & Digital Transformation

## The Challenge

DVOC Institute, a leading ed-tech platform offering digital marketing certifications and courses, was struggling to reach their target audience effectively. They had:

- **Limited brand awareness** in a crowded ed-tech space
- **Outdated web presence** that didn't convert visitors into leads
- **No structured social media strategy** or content pipeline
- **High customer acquisition costs** with traditional advertising methods
- **Difficulty reaching** both students and working professionals simultaneously

Despite having quality courses and expertise, DVOC couldn't scale their student enrollment without significant marketing spend.

---

## Our Solution

We positioned Vaelo as their **full-service growth partner**, combining:

1. **Brand & Web Strategy** — Rebuild digital presence with conversion-focused design
2. **AI-Native Content Production** — Scale social media presence with AI-generated video content and graphics
3. **Performance Marketing** — Data-driven Meta & Google ads targeting multiple audience segments
4. **Funnel Optimization** — Strategic lead capture and nurturing

### The Approach
Rather than relying on expensive video production, we leveraged **AI-native creation tools** to produce 10+ variations of course-focused ad creatives weekly. This allowed rapid testing and optimization at a fraction of traditional production costs.

---

## What We Delivered

### 1. **Website Redesign**
- Rebuilt on Wix Studio with conversion-focused layout
- Clear course pathways and CTAs
- Optimized landing pages for each course vertical
- Integrated lead capture forms with email automation

### 2. **Content & Creative System**
- **Social Media Strategy** — 4-post weekly calendar aligned with course launches
- **AI Video Production** — 50+ promotional videos using Kling & Higgsfield
- **Ad Creatives** — 200+ variations of course ads and testimonial graphics
- **Email Sequences** — Automated nurture campaigns for leads

### 3. **Paid Media Campaigns**
- **Meta Ads** — Targeted campaigns for students (18-35) and professionals (25-45)
- **Google Search** — High-intent keyword campaigns for "digital marketing course"
- **Audience Segmentation** — Separate campaigns by course level (beginner, advanced) and learner type

### 4. **Lead Management**
- Custom lead qualification workflow
- CRM integration for automatic follow-ups
- Weekly performance reports and optimization
