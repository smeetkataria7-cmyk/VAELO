# Marigold Miraya — Building a Real Estate Pipeline at Scale

## The Problem

Marigold Miraya, a premium real estate developer, had quality properties but was struggling with the fundamentals of modern real estate marketing:

- **Limited social media presence** — Not reaching potential buyers where they search and research
- **Inconsistent lead generation** — No systematic way to capture interested buyers
- **Expensive customer acquisition** — Traditional advertising channels weren't delivering ROI
- **No digital property showcase** — Online presence didn't highlight their projects effectively
- **Competing in a crowded market** — Struggling to stand out among other developers

In real estate, perception and lead quality matter as much as lead quantity. Marigold Miraya needed a complete digital system, not just ads.

---

## Our Solution

We built a **full-stack real estate marketing system** combining social proof, targeted advertising, and lead capture:

1. **Social Media Strategy** — Build authority and reach through consistent, property-focused content
2. **Meta Ads Campaigns** — Target high-intent buyers at every stage of their search journey
3. **Website Development** — Create a digital showroom for projects with lead capture optimization
4. **Content Production** — AI-powered property videos, virtual tours, market insights
5. **Lead Management** — Systematic follow-up to convert inquiries into viewings and sales

The key: Real estate buyers research extensively. We needed to be visible at every stage.

---

  - High-income individuals (target income bracket)
  - Real estate enthusiasts and previous home buyers
  - Geographic targeting (neighborhoods where projects are located)
  - Interest-based (luxury real estate, investment properties, home improvement)

### 3. **Website Development**

### 4. **AI-Powered Content Production**

## How It Worked

### Real Estate Buyers Are Researchers
Most real estate purchases involve extensive research. A buyer might spend weeks looking at properties, reading reviews, comparing neighborhoods, analyzing investments. We needed to be visible at every step.

**The Solution:** Consistent social media content kept Marigold Miraya top-of-mind. Meta ads ensured we reached them at the right moment with the right property. The website closed the deal with a seamless lead capture experience.

### Content = Authority
In real estate, buyers want to work with developers they trust. By consistently sharing market insights, buyer testimonials, and transparent information, we positioned Marigold Miraya as the knowledgeable, trustworthy choice.

### Efficiency Through Automation
The lead management system meant that qualified leads were automatically nurtured. Follow-ups happened on schedule. Hot leads were flagged for immediate sales attention. No lead fell through the cracks.
